1. Handlebars: Uses two back straps and one step stabilizing strap to attach the bag to flat or drop bars. Recommended max carrying weight is 3 Kilos.

2. Racks: Thanks to the rigid construction the bag can be attached to most rear and front racks using the two bottom straps.
